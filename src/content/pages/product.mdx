<Rows>
<Row title="The Library Layer">

A continuously evolving library of expert-developed mathematics teaching,
learning, and assessment materials. Materials are authored in PreTeXt, an
open-source semantic markup language software, enabling publication
simultaneously as web, app, chatbot, or print from a single source. Formative
mathematics assessment technology STACK is embedded.

</Row>
<Row title="The Versioning Middleware Layer">

The core innovation, a middleware developed by IDEMS' mathematicians and
mathematical scientists. Rather than storing adaptations as separate copies, the
variant layer encodes every contextual change (software tool, language, dataset,
local examples, accessibility accommodations) as a recombine-able data element
with semantic meaning. An innovation anywhere becomes an innovation that can be
used everywhere in a system with no duplication and no siloed forks.

</Row>
<Row title="The Publishing Layer">

With the support of expert AI agents, educators at national, regional, school, or
classroom level compile context-specific textbooks (with permissions governed by
stakeholders) out of a single textbooks platform hub.

</Row>
</Rows>
